From the Editor (CEO)

When I started Confection Extras, I envisioned a place where I could connect customers from all over the world to their favorite sweets from yesteryear and beyond. Gone are the days of searching high and low for nostalgic classics or imported delights. We take the guesswork out of candy shopping by offering a seamless ordering experience. Whether your next office gathering is in dire need of scrumptious chocolates or you're on the hunt for after-dinner mints to match the color of your wedding party, we've got you covered! No matter your craving or location, Confection Extras has plenty of sweets, both new and familiar, waiting to be unwrapped.
